🛠️ MGNREGA Permissible Works

The Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act supports a wide range of rural works focused on sustainable development. Below are water-related activities that are aligned with our mission:

💧 Water Conservation

Construction of check dams, percolation tanks, and contour trenches to conserve water resources.

🌱 Drought Proofing

Tree planting, agroforestry, and building of shelterbelts to increase resilience to droughts.

🚰 Irrigation Channels

Construction and maintenance of minor irrigation canals and field channels to support agriculture.

🏞️ Renovation of Water Bodies

Desilting and restoring ponds, tanks, and traditional step wells to revive water ecosystems.

🌾 Land Development

Levelling, terracing, and soil amendment activities to enhance land productivity.
