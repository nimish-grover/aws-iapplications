About Us

Talent Oriented Public School, Sursura, was established in 2009 with a mission to provide quality education in a nurturing and inclusive environment. Located in the rural heartland of Kishangarh block, Ajmer district, Rajasthan, our school serves students from Grade 1 to 8, with an attached pre-primary section.

As a co-educational institution, we are dedicated to fostering academic excellence and holistic development. With Hindi as our primary medium of instruction, we ensure that students receive education that is accessible, engaging, and rooted in strong values. Our approach goes beyond textbooks—we emphasize critical thinking, creativity, and practical learning, helping students prepare for a future full of opportunities.

At Talent Oriented Public School, we believe that every child deserves the best start in life. Through dedicated faculty, structured learning, and a focus on individual growth, we continue to inspire young minds to achieve their full potential.

Learning Ecosystem

We are committed to creating a dynamic and interactive learning environment where students feel encouraged to explore and grow. Our classrooms are designed to be student-friendly, ensuring that each child receives personalized attention. The school promotes a blend of academic rigor and experiential learning, allowing students to develop not just knowledge, but also essential life skills. Join a community of determined, resilient, and goal-driven young achievers.

School Infrastructure

The finest school in town, offering exceptional faculty and best-in-class facilities. Our school campus is equipped with essential facilities that support both academic and extracurricular development. The private school building houses 10 spacious classrooms, all in excellent condition. Additional rooms are allocated for non-teaching activities, ensuring a well-rounded learning experience.

Navodaya Selection

At Talent Oriented Public School, we take pride in preparing students for the prestigious Navodaya Vidyalaya entrance exam. Our structured training modules and special coaching sessions ensure that students are well-equipped with the knowledge and confidence required to excel in this competitive examination. The school has consistently nurtured and produced Navodaya scholars.
